# Nine One Competitor Matrix

## Summary

| Competitor | Primary category | Main users | Strongest capability | Weakness / gap | Nine One opportunity |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Playtomic | Racket-sports marketplace + club SaaS | Players, clubs, coaches | Open matches, booking, levels, club operations | Fee/support complaints, rating trust issues, platform dependency | Padel-native booking plus transparent skill, smart coaching, and club-owned community |
| MATCHi | Racket-sports booking + venue platform | Players, venues | Clean booking, multi-sport venue operations | Less visible coaching/social depth; public pricing opaque | Better group coordination, player improvement, and venue analytics |
| CourtReserve | Club management SaaS | Club operators, members, staff | Deep operations: reservations, memberships, events, billing, POS | Less marketplace/social/player improvement focus | Simpler padel-ready operating system with smart coaching and community |
| Padel Mates | Racket-sports booking/community app | Players, clubs, coaches | Booking, match discovery, chat, split payments, level feedback | Reliability/rating complaints; smaller visible reach | Reliable padel community layer with video-assisted progression |
| SwingVision | AI video/coaching app | Tennis/pickleball players, coaches | AI stats, highlights, line calling, goals | No booking/club operations; padel support Unknown | Combine venue booking with padel-specific AI coaching |
| Strava | Fitness social network | Athletes, clubs, subscribers | Social proof, segments, progress loops, clubs, challenges | Not court-sport specific; club features debated | Make each padel match a shareable progress object |
| Hudl | Sports video/team analysis | Teams, coaches, athletes, recruiters | Video analysis, smart cameras, team learning, recruiting | Too heavy/team-oriented for recreational padel; not booking-native | Lightweight Hudl-for-padel tied to courts, coaches, and clubs |

## Market pattern

| Pattern | Evidence | Strategic implication |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Booking is commoditizing | Playtomic, MATCHi, CourtReserve, and Padel Mates all support booking and payments. | Nine One should not position as only another booking app. |
| Match formation is still painful | Reviews mention rating quality, payment holds, support issues, and WhatsApp reliance. | Build trusted match quality, reliable payment state, and structured group workflows. |
| Smart coaching is separate from booking | SwingVision and Hudl have strong analysis, but do not own padel venue booking. | Combine real sessions with AI/human coaching loops. |
| Club operators need utilization and retention | CourtReserve and Playtomic emphasize reporting, payments, and club management. | Tie venue dashboards to player lifecycle: first booking, repeat play, lesson conversion, membership. |
| Social proof drives retention | Strava shows the durability of feed, goals, leaderboards, clubs, and challenges. | Turn match participation into achievement, reputation, progression, and social identity. |
